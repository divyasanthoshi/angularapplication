The department is requiring providers to obtain approval from the local government entity, HOA, and/or Landlord, or sign an attestation acknowledging their responsibility for ensuring compliance with their local government entities, HOA, and/or Landlord.

Approval or attestation documents must be submitted as part of the application. The approval can be in the form of a letter on letterhead or an official form from the local government entity, HOA, and/or Landlord.
